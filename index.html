    Kyreen White, is a Junior attending the University of New Mexico, to graduate in Spring of 2026. She is pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Geography with minors in Sustainability Studies and Earth and Planetary Sciences. She is currently working for the UNM Housing office as a Student Safety employee. She is the Community Engagement Intern for Lobo Gardens, helping with Lobo Gardens and connecting with the University of New Mexico Community. Keeping busy she is involved in many environmental organizations as the president of Lobo Gardens Club and the Director of Finance for UNM LEAF (UNM Leaders for Environmental Action and Foresight). She enjoys learning about the interaction between the earth systems and takes an interest in studying soil health.
